HARONA and its Partners

HARONA NGO builds strategic alliances with organizations and businesses that share its vision for sustainable, inclusive, and resilient development. These partnerships allow us to strengthen local capacities, introduce innovations adapted to local realities, and maximize the impact of projects on vulnerable communities. By collaborating with various stakeholders—institutions, NGOs, and private companies—HARONA promotes complementary expertise and the pooling of resources to achieve concrete and lasting results.

Collaboration with MAD’AROM

With MAD’AROM, a social enterprise specializing in the valorization of aromatic plants and spices from Madagascar, HARONA promotes the empowerment of small-scale producers, the creation of sustainable value chains, and the generation of decent incomes for rural communities. This partnership combines technical expertise, community support, and commercial outlets, ensuring a win-win model where local development and economic opportunities are harmoniously linked.

Collaboration with SmartSaha

In partnership with SmartSaha, a digital platform for agricultural monitoring and traceability, HARONA integrates digital technologies to serve producers and communities. This collaboration improves access to climate data, agricultural risk management, and creates transparent and equitable market opportunities. Together, HARONA and SmartSaha are building an innovative approach that strengthens the resilience of small-scale producers while accelerating their inclusion in the digital and sustainable economy.
