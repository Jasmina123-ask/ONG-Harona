HARONA and its Partners

HARONA NGO builds strategic alliances with organizations and businesses that share its vision for sustainable, inclusive, and resilient development. These partnerships allow us to strengthen local capacities, introduce innovations adapted to local realities, and maximize the impact of projects on vulnerable communities. By collaborating with various stakeholders—institutions, NGOs, and private companies—HARONA promotes complementary expertise and the pooling of resources to achieve concrete and lasting results.

Empowering Communities – Partnership with MAD’AROM

At HARONA, we believe that rural empowerment requires strong and inclusive partnerships. In collaboration with MAD’AROM, we work with farming families to provide training in post-harvest processing, quality improvement, and value addition, ensuring that agricultural products reach markets at higher standards and generate greater income. Together, we go beyond agriculture by supporting access to clean water and essential nutrition, addressing critical needs for community well-being. This partnership also prioritizes the empowerment of women and youth, equipping them with entrepreneurial skills and opportunities to participate actively in local economic growth. By aligning HARONA’s community development mission with MAD’AROM’s expertise in sustainable value chains, we create a powerful model that transforms rural communities into resilient, self-reliant actors of their own development.

Sustainable Agriculture & Digital Innovation – SmartSaha Collaboration

At HARONA, we believe that community resilience starts with sustainable farming systems. Through our collaboration with SmartSaha, we bring digital agricultural technologies closer to smallholder farmers, enabling them to improve productivity, anticipate climate risks, and connect to markets. The platform offers practical tools such as weather alerts, advisory services, and digital traceability, empowering farmers to adopt climate-smart practices while ensuring transparency for buyers. By combining grassroots action with digital innovation, we strengthen value chains that are both resilient and inclusive, helping rural communities in Madagascar thrive in a changing environment.
